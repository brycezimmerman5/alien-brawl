Alien Brawl

Alien Brawl – Fight. Survive. Dominate.

Step into the ultimate intergalactic arena in Alien Brawl, a fast-paced multiplayer battle game where chaos meets strategy. Team up or go solo as you face off against alien enemies – or each other – in wave-based survival or intense PvP combat.

⚔️ Game Features:

  • Survival Mode: Fight off endless waves of aliens with melee weapons and power-ups.
  • PvP Mode: Challenge your friends in close-quarters combat with skill-based attacks.
  • Unique Abilities: Dash, strike, and unleash ranged powers to outplay your foes.
  • Power-Ups: Grab the legendary sword mid-match to turn the tide of battle.
  • Stylized Combat: Fluid movement, satisfying physics, and a clean UI.

Whether you're smashing aliens with your squad or brawling for dominance in the arena, Alien Brawl delivers non-stop action and epic moments every match.
